03 — Typography
Inter, heavy and tight
Creme uses a bold, tightly-tracked sans for impact. Inter at weights 700–800 with negative letter-spacing gives the same punchy, modern editorial feel. No serif — this direction is clean and contemporary.
Inter
Inter across the board, leaning heavy (700–800) for titles with tight negative tracking, regular (400) for body. Uppercase amber labels (like Creme's "CRUNCHY") mark categories.
